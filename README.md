# SubscryptionMangment 🚀

A privacy focused iOS application built with **SwiftUI** to manage, track, and organize all your digital subscriptions in one place. Whether it's streaming, software, or gym memberships, never let a renewal catch you off guard again.

## 🔒 Privacy First: 100% Local Storage
Unlike other trackers, **SubscryptionMangment stores all data locally on your device.** * No cloud databases.
* No account requirements.
* Your credentials and member lists never leave your phone.

## ✨ Core Features

* **Universal Subscription Tracking:** Manage any service (YouTube, Netflix, Spotify, Gyms, etc.) with custom renewal dates.
* **Family Group Coordinator:** Perfect for managing shared "Family Plans." Track who is in which slot and when their specific access expires.
* **Smart Alerts:** Color-coded urgency badges (Yellow for 2 days, Green for 10+ days) help you prioritize upcoming renewals.
* **One-Tap Communication:** Integrated **WhatsApp shortcut** to instantly message group members when their subscription is ending.
* **Credential Vault:** Keep track of the specific emails and passwords associated with each shared account.
* **CSV Export:** Generate a professional spreadsheet of all your accounts and members for easy backup or external auditing.
